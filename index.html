About Red Pandas

World Wildlife

Fun Facts

  • They are one of the only animals that can climb down a tree head first
  • They eat 20-30% of their body weight in bamboo everyday
  • Red pandas sleep 17-22 hours a day
  • Red Pandas quack like ducks
  • They can stand on their hind legs
  • They are native to Eastern Himalayans and SouthWestern China
